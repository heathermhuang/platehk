## Why this matters

- The old `all` experience merged child datasets on the fly and then special-cased legacy overlap removal.
- The new model is closer to a real database:
  - `plate` is the deduplicated entity
  - `auction record` is the historical event
- Same plate across multiple auctions is still preserved.
- Same auction row duplicated by bilingual/source overlap is now collapsed.
